Explore the extensive dining options the Hilltop Manor Hotel has to offer

Our stylish contemporary Bistro Restaurant is one of the finest restaurants Waterford has to offer. The Bistro Restaurant incorporates several unique design features with each of its six sections designed with a different theme and style of decor. Our bistro has one entire corner given over to glass which offers an abundance of natural light and superb views over the town of Ardmore. Throughout the restaurant, the combination of wooden floors, stylish furnishings and modern glass and tableware lends an upmarket, contemporary atmosphere at this 4 Star Hotel.. The menu is varied and tempting to both the eye as well as the palate. Our Table D'Hote Menu is available in the Bistro on Friday and Saturday evenings from 6pm until 9.45pm. An extensive wine list offering a selection of wines from all over the world is also available. Dinner is served in the Bistro Restaurant in Ardmore on Friday and Saturday nights only. From Sunday to Thursday inclusive, dinner is served in our contemporary Hilltop Bar.

With 82 spacious and stylishly furnished en-suite bedrooms, comfort is foremost in mind for our guests at the Hilltop. Each room is equipped with all of the modern conveniences you would expect to see from any premier quality hotel. Bedrooms have wall mounted, multi-channel plasma TVs, Free Wi-Fi internet access and air-conditioning. Our superior bedrooms have one double and one single bed, each room is decorated with dark woods and purple tones to help you relax during your stay. Superior Rooms are a minimum of 24.45m squared you can upgrade to a Deluxe Executive Suite for only 30 euro extra per night. Hilltop suites have all the facilities of our superior rooms as well as a few luxuries to make your stay even more memorable. Executive Suites include a luxurious double bed and a spacious lounge with private seating and work area. Executive Suites are a minimum of 30.35m squared The superb An Chaiseal Suite is fitted with a King size bed, Jacuzzi bath, 2 plasma screen televisions, luxurious seating area and table for private dining. An additional bonus with this room is the private decked veranda with a spectacular view of the beach. Please contact us directly to book a superior room or the Presidential Suite.

If you are looking for a luxurious peaceful hotel to stay look no further

The five star hotel in Waterford is beautifully located overlooking the Ardmore beach. We are open year round but we thrive in the summer time due to the beach and warm weather. Don't be mistaken though, there is still much to do all year round. With expert concierge services and genuine pride for this part of hidden Ireland we can direct you towards activities suitable for rain, sun, culture, children or the actively inclined.This includes exploring Ardmore with its sandy beach, traditional music and thatched cottages. Youghal, just 15 minutes away, is one of the best examples of a Norman walled port in Ireland.
